Cancellation policy

Please provide a minimum of 48 hours notice before cancelling a session, if you fail to give sufficient notice you will lose your session.

Freezing sessions

Due to the nature of our work together. I do not encourage breaks in our sessions, however, i understand life. so I will grant a maximum halt of 3 months in exceptional circumstances with proof of circumstances. Exceptional circumstance includes; Bereavement; Health issues and Legal matters.

Fees and payment

Payment for package must be made in advance. Payment plans can be discussed if necessary.

Boundaries and Relationships

All our conversations are confidential howvere if i feel yiu are a danger to yourself or others i am legally bound to contact relevant departments. I am available to you in our sessions however do not permit contact outside of our allotted time.

Termination of sessions on either side

If either of us feels that the sessions are not working, we will have a session outside of your package, to discuss if it is possible to move forward, or if we must terminate our work together. If you can no longer continue with sessions for a reason that does not fall within the exceptional circumstances, you will not be reimbursed and you must give a minimum of 1-week notice.
