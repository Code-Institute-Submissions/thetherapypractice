What makes my help so valid?

credentials

I hold a certificate & Diploma in integrative body psychotherapy. I am registered with UKCP, CABP (Chiron Association of Body Psychotherapists).I have worked for a number of Employee Assisted Programmes and am especially experienced in working with issues of work place bullying and the power dynamics I am also a qualified and practicing occupational therapist & hypnotherapist. It is a combination of all 3 professions that enables me to bring my unique approach to my practice enabling me to provide a tailor made intervention for my clients. I have worked for 10 years in a leading mental health charity in inner city london with a variety of issues where I was managing a prescribed drugs project and successfully establishing withdrawal programmes to gradually reduce & eventually suspend prescribed medication altogether working closely with other health professionals. I have worked in a variety of settings ranging from voluntary sector, health, education and the private sector. I am passionate about supporting healthy bonds between parents & young people. I BELIEVE IN YOUNG PEOPE having opportunities to reach their potential. I have worked for over 2 years in a private Eating Disorders establishment.

Client struggles I have helped solve!

I am particularly drawn to working with inter-cultural issues, depression, parenting challenges, family breakdown, couples therapy, feelings of low self esteem, anger and bereavement. I focus on these issues in particular because of my own experiences with them and becuase i believe that these issues are at the crux of humanity. Many of them are things we will all face and can either make or break us. I am very proud of my culture and learning everyday about the mental effects of being a non-white proffesional in the western world. This is something else my clients are always grateful for my insight with.
