Welcome to the therapy practice

I’m a first generation, UK born, Punjabi woman. I speak fluent Punjabi.  I am a mother of 2 young adults. I live in London. I believe we are all on our journey, forever evolving and each experience provides us with the opportunity to grow. ‘The starting point of our journey of working together is our relationship’

Self development is a never ending journey, life is constantly testing us and often we feel that we have to wait for a crisis before repairing ourselves. This is not true!! Prevention is almost always better than cause. In any case, I am here to help!
