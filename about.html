My first experience with Psychotherapy

Not many people know.. but if you are training to become a psychotherapist you must also undergo intensive psychotherapy so that you can really practice what you preach! Ironically, one of my biggest regrets is training so young to become a pyschotherapist! i was only 23 years old, lost my mother only a year prior and being PLUNGED into intesive therapy meant that i couldnt just 'get on with things' i HAD to face all my baggage! Despite this being tough, i truly believe it saved me.
