Virtual Psychotherapy

I embarked on my Psychotherapy career when the world was different, technology was nowhere near as developed as it is now! This of course has its many benefits, however in my opinion, contributes to our heightened levels of stress and anxiety and lowered level of deep human connections. I am going to meet you in the middle, we will discuss everything in depth, however we will do this bia a virtual platform. Since COVID 19, I hope that we are collectively , more aware of how important it is to look after our inneer world AND how much we can still achieve virtually. I realise how tough it is to make sure we prioritise ourselves, so i wanted to remove the hassle of travel and connect with you in your preferred space. I will carry out sessions virtually using platforms such as ;facetime; skype and zoom .
